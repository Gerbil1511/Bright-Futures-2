What Happens Next?

Confirmation Email

You'll receive an email confirmation of your booking request within the next hour. Please check your spam folder if you don't see it.

Ambassador Assignment

Within 2 business days, we'll match you with one of our trained ambassadors based on your session requirements and location.

Final Details

Your assigned ambassador will contact you to confirm all session details, discuss any special requirements, and answer your questions.

Pre-Session Materials

One week before your session, we'll send you preparatory materials to help get the children excited and ready for the visit.
